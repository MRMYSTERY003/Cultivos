ABOUT THE APP

    1. On entering the App, select a pic of your plant from your device gallery.
    2. Then load it into the App.
    3. It analyzes the disease and displays suggestions that can be followed.
    4. Look after the plant and follow the suggested ideas in order to see the best results.

    The app Cultivos is designed in such a way that farmers can know more about plant diseases and can choose suitable crop for their soil and useful in checking weather.
    It gives us the details of the weather for next 10 days so that the farmer can plan for sowing and cultivation of their crops. This app project is based on Artificial Intelligence and Convolutional Neural Network (CNN).

    Food security for billions of people on earth requires minimizing crop damage by timely detection of diseases.
    Developing methods for detection of plant diseases serves the dual purpose of increasing crop yield and reducing pesticide use without knowing about the proper disease.
